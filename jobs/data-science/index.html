The job

Our product depends on sound statistical methods to do its job. Data science is not a 'nice to have' for us; its fundamental to our ability to solve problems for our customers. Your core responsibilities will include:

  • Improving data quality of core product, including:
    • implementing, evaluating, and deploying generative models in production environments
    • improving the usability and explainability of synthetic datasets for data consumers
    • implementing, evaluating, and deploying methods for limiting exposure risk in synthetic datasets
  • Interfacing with customer technical / analysts teams and shaping product to solve their problems in scalable ways
  • Heavily shaping the direction of the product as a very early team member, and collaborate with technical and non-technical team members and founders

We're a fully remote company, so its ok to work from anywhere. This role is eligible for both salary + equity compensation.
