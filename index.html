Share statistically similar data instead of the real thing.

Simple redaction isn't enough to protect your users. Subsalt can generate lookalike records that are similar to but unassociated to your dataset, and makes it easy to safely share with anyone without extensive legal reviews or additional engineering effort.

1.

Connect your sample data

Subsalt derives statistical models of your data without storing or exporting raw records.

  • Read from all popular SQL and NoSQL databases
  • Hosted or deployable within your VPC

2.

Generate lookalike tables

Audiences will have access to a lookalike dataset modeled after your underlying data, but with built-in privacy guarantees.

3.

Start sharing

Once enabled, internal or external partners can access your lookalike data sources via managed database queries, APIs, and FTP.

Subsalt ensures your privacy constraints are enforced with every query, regardless of the access method.
